# Prebloom UI Propositions

*For discussion with UI designer — 2026-01-31*

---

## Proposition 1: "The McKinsey Report"

**Concept:** Output looks like you paid €50K for a consulting engagement

**Vibe:** Clean, authoritative, data-driven. Navy/white/gold. PDF-worthy.

**Report structure:**
- Cover page with idea title + date + confidence score
- Executive Summary (1 paragraph, the verdict)
- Market Context (what Intake found)
- Opportunity Analysis (Catalyst's bull case)
- Risk Assessment (Fire's bear case)  
- Strategic Recommendation (Synthesis verdict)
- Appendix: Full methodology explanation

**Target buyer appeal:** Banks — "We offer this to SME clients before they apply for startup loans"

---

## Proposition 2: "The Pitch Deck Validator"

**Concept:** Output is formatted like feedback on a pitch — what VCs actually look for

**Vibe:** Modern, sharp, founder-friendly. Scoring rubric visible.

**Report structure:**
- Overall Score (like Y Combinator's internal rating)
- Problem Score + feedback
- Solution Score + feedback
- Market Score + feedback
- Business Model Score + feedback
- Team/Founder Score + feedback
- "Would we fund this?" verdict with reasoning

**Target buyer appeal:** Telecoms/accelerators — "Part of our startup accelerator program toolkit"

---

## Proposition 3: "The War Room Debrief" ⭐ SELECTED FOR MVP

**Concept:** You're getting the internal memo from the investment committee

**Vibe:** Dark, serious, insider-access feel. Like reading classified docs.

**Report structure:**
- CLASSIFICATION: [PASS/FAIL/CONDITIONAL]
- SITUATION BRIEF (the idea)
- BULL CASE (Agent Catalyst's full argument)
- BEAR CASE (Agent Fire's full argument)
- COMMITTEE DECISION (Synthesis + reasoning)
- RECOMMENDED ACTIONS (numbered, specific)
- ATTACHMENTS: Full transcripts

**Why it's compelling:** Feels exclusive, like you're getting access to how the real decisions get made

---

## Proposition 4: "The Living Report"

**Concept:** Not just a static report — it's interactive and evolves

**Vibe:** Dashboard-like. Can drill down, ask follow-ups.

**Features:**
- Chat to submit idea
- Report generates with collapsible sections
- "Ask a follow-up" button on any section
- "Generate Business Model Canvas" button
- "Create 90-day validation plan" button
- Save/share/export

**Target buyer appeal:** Enterprise platform play — sticky, upsell potential
